Tatsiana Kulakevich is a Ph.D. Candidate in political science at Rutgers University. Her analyses appeared in media and academic journals, including The Washington Post, Belarus Digest, and East European Politics Societies and Cultures. Her specialties include International Relations, Eastern European politics, and Quantitative Methods. She holds an M.A. in International Relations from New York University.
